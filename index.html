Why did I make this website?

I think around 2022-23, I got hooked on HTML and messed with it a lot. I wanted to put them for everyone to see, so I looked up how and stumbled upon github. And at that same time I made the first itteration of this website , which you can find here , I will admit it's pretty cringe-y now, so I recommend you don't go there :p

What you are seeing here is the second itteration, that until now (late-june 2026) was forgotten within the code. I suppose before I left for home I never got to update the website.
